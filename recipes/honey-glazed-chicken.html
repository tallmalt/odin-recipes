Honey Glazed Chicken

Description

This honey chicken recipe is quick and easy to make.

It is a light meal best served with steamed rice and your favourite sides.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, but into bite-sized pieces

Steps

  1. Whisk honey, soy sauce and red pepper flakes ina a bowl, set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes.
  3. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
  4. Serve over hot rice and your favourite side dishes.
